ExplorR is bringing back the authentic traveling experience that we once had

Hi we are a startup team to create a new traveling app

Long before trip-advisor and google maps became a thing, travelling overseas was an adventure -
what lies ahead in our destination remains a mystery for us to uncover. Yet with so much information
available on the internet today, we tend to over-plan our travel itinerary with the 'fear of missing out'
certain places. The refreshing excitement of discovery is thus lost. We want to bring that excitement
of unplanned uncovering of points of interests back into modern travel with ExplorR.

You will like what we are doing if you are

  • an indepandent solo traveler who are not paying high prenium tour agency fee
  • an adventurer who are tired of planned itinerary and want more spontaneity
  • an explorer who are looking to find the hidden gems in the city

We are designing our product to meet your needs

  1. End to end experience design to faciliate trip planning,POIs discovery and navigation
  2. Curated Point of interests and co-created by the community
  3. More exciting features to reinvent the traveling experience
